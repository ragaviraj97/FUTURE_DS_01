# 📊 AI Job Trends Analysis Project

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes the **AI Job Market Trends** using Excel and Power BI to uncover insights about salary, job growth, remote work, and automation risk.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze AI job demand and growth trends  
- Identify high-paying industries and roles  
- Understand automation risk across jobs  
- Explore remote work opportunities  
- Provide actionable insights for career growth  

---

## 📂 Project Files
- 📁 `AI_Jobs_Dataset.xlsx` → Raw dataset used for analysis  
- 📁 `AI_Job_Trends.pbix` → Power BI dashboard file  
- 📁 `AI_Job_Trends_Report.pdf` → Insights & storytelling report  

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning & Preprocessing)  
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)  

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 🔹 Overall Summary
The AI job market is rapidly growing, with around **30,000 job openings** and nearly **50% remote roles**, showing high demand and flexibility.

### 🔹 Salary Insights
- IT and Finance industries offer the **highest salaries**  
- High AI impact roles tend to pay more  

### 🔹 Industry Growth
- IT is the **fastest-growing industry**  
- Healthcare & Manufacturing provide **stable growth**  

### 🔹 Remote Work
- Nearly **50% jobs are remote**  
- Global hiring is increasing  

### 🔹 Automation Risk
- Repetitive jobs have **high automation risk**  
- Upskilling is necessary for career security  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features (Power BI)
- Salary distribution by industry  
- AI impact level analysis  
- Job growth trends  
- Automation risk visualization  
- Interactive slicers (Industry, Location, Job Title)  

---

## 🔮 Conclusion
The AI job market is expanding rapidly with strong demand for technical skills. While high-paying opportunities are increasing, automation risk is also rising. Continuous upskilling in AI and data is essential for long-term career success.
